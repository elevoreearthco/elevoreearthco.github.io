About the Game

Enter the mysterious world of Elevore Earth in the most immersive VR RPG experience ever created. As Slayer, a level one playtester, you'll uncover dark secrets hidden within a world where NPCs have gone missing, and leveling up is impossible. Explore, fight, and survive in a fully interactive environment with limitless possibilities.

Explore Unique Worlds

Experience beautiful, interactive environments in Elevore Earth, each with its own unique atmosphere and hidden secrets to discover.

Engage in Intense Combat

Battle enemies in fast-paced, immersive combat as you journey through the world, using weapons and tactics to gain the upper hand.

Uncover Hidden Secrets

Unravel the mysteries behind the missing NPCs and discover the dark truth that lies beneath the surface of the game world.

Fully Interactive Environment

Almost every object in the environment is interactable, allowing you to shape your experience and interact with the world in new ways.
