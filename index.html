About the Game

Enter the mysterious world of Elevore Earth in the most immersive VR RPG experience ever created. As Slayer, a level one playtester, you'll uncover dark secrets hidden within a world where NPCs have gone missing, and leveling up is impossible. Explore, fight, and survive in a fully interactive environment with limitless possibilities.
